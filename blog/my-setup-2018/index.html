Tips & notes

  • To directly run tmux with alacritty you can edit your DesktopEntry like that: bash [Desktop Entry] [...] Exec=env WAYLAND_DISPLAY= alacritty -e tmux [...] - Use Visual Code Studio from FlatHub to have a Microsoft-free compiled binary. Because binaries given by Microsoft are not open source. - My dotfiles are on Gitlab. - I don’t have standing desk, but I have a nice seat. My back never hurts. - My terminal and editor have grey & white (monochrome for editor) colorschemes. I advize you to try it if you have regular headcaches. - I’m self hosting some services like: Shaarli, TinyTinyRss, Nextcloud, RSS-Bridge and more. The most important part, buy a nebulizer diffuser it’s amazing. My favorite essential oils are “Pepper mint”, “Tea tree” & “Niaouli”. Feel free to leave comments and share your setup here.
